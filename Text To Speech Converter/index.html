Text To Speech Converter

This is a free web app that allows you to convert any text into an audio file. Simply type or paste your text into the box, choose your preferred voice, and click 'Listen'. The app will generate an audio file that you can listen to. It's a great way to convert long texts or e-books into audio files for easy listening, and to help you get the correct pronounciation of a word.
